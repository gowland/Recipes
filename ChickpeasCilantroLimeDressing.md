# Chickpeas with Cilantro-Lime Dressing

## INGREDIENTS
### for the salad
* 4 cans chickpeas
* 1 red onion
* 1 red pepper
* 2 zucchini
### for the dressing
* 4 oz. cilantro
* 1/4 cup fresh lime juice or white balsamic vinegar
* 1/4 cup olive oil
* 1 to 2 cloves garlic
* 2 teaspoons horse radish
* 1/2 teaspoon turmeric (optional)
* 1/4 teaspoon salt or more to taste
* freshly cracked black pepper to taste

## INSTRUCTIONS
1. Place the cilantro, lime or vinegar, olive oil, garlic, horse radish, turmeric, salt and pepper in a food processor or blender. Pur√©e until smooth. Taste. Adjust dressing with more salt, pepper, lime, oil, etc.
2. Place cooked chickpeas in a large bowl. Add minced onion, pepper, and zucchini. Toss with dressing to taste.
